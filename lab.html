Where Discovery Happens Daily

The Drug and Vaccine Development Laboratory (DVDLab) is a fully equipped, biosafety-compliant research facility at GLA University, Mathura. Designed to support cutting-edge work in drug discovery, cancer biology, environmental toxicology, and vaccine development, the lab integrates both computational and wet-lab research under one roof.

The lab operates under Biosafety Level 1 & 2 protocols, with trained research scholars, a dedicated PI cabin for strategic guidance, and a collaborative workspace that fosters innovation and scientific rigour.
