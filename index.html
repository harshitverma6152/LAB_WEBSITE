Drug and Vaccine Development Laboratory

Accelerating interventions for infectious diseases, chronic conditions, and emerging health threats.

A multidisciplinary research and translational hub dedicated to discovering, designing, and advancing safe, effective therapeutics and vaccines from concept to clinic. Combining molecular biology, medicinal chemistry, immunology, and preclinical pharmacology, the laboratory bridges basic science with regulatory-grade development workflows.

Infectious Diseases

Focus on pathogen biology, vaccine antigen development, and new antimicrobial strategies to overcome resistance in mycobacterial diseases.

Cancer Research

Our cancer work spans target discovery, novel therapeutic design, and preclinical evaluation of small molecules and biologics.
